About Leo's Club

Leo's Club is the youth arm of Lions Clubs International — one of the largest and most respected service organizations in the world, with a presence in over 200 countries and territories. The name "LEO" stands for Leadership, Experience, and Opportunity — three pillars that define the mission of empowering young people aged 12 to 30 to become compassionate leaders and active agents of positive change in their communities.

Founded in 1957, the Leo Club program has grown to include over 7,000 clubs worldwide, with hundreds of thousands of members collectively dedicating millions of volunteer hours each year. Leo Clubs operate under the guidance of a sponsoring Lions Club, providing young members with a structured environment to develop organizational skills, community awareness, and a spirit of volunteerism.
