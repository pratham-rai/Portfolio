Venue Management & POS Platform

The Courtyard

Comprehensive venue management & POS platform with real-time court bookings, membership tiers, dynamic peak-pricing engines, QR check-ins, and secure administrative accounting.

Next.js MongoDB Atlas Stripe OAuth 2.0

The Challenge & Vision

Modern sports venues struggle with fragmented booking systems, manual pricing adjustments, and paper-based check-in workflows. The Courtyard was conceived as a unified, production-ready platform that collapses venue management, point-of-sale, membership administration, and real-time analytics into a single cohesive system.

The vision was to build software that venue operators could deploy out-of-the-box — handling everything from peak/off-peak dynamic pricing to split-billing POS transactions and QR-based member check-ins — all backed by robust concurrency controls and secure payment processing.

Architecture & Key Features

  • Concurrency Protection with MongoDB Transaction Locking
    Implements multi-document ACID transactions to prevent double-booking, ensuring slot integrity even under high concurrent request loads during peak hours.
  • Dynamic Peak / Off-Peak Pricing Engine
    Algorithmically adjusts court rental prices based on configurable time-of-day rules, day-of-week demand curves, and seasonal multipliers — maximizing venue revenue automatically.
  • QR Code Check-In System
    Generates unique, time-bound QR tokens per booking that are scanned at entry. Validates membership tier, booking window, and payment status in real-time.
  • Multi-Tier Membership Management
    Supports Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum membership levels with configurable discount matrices, priority booking windows, and auto-renewal via Stripe subscriptions.
  • Split Billing POS System
    Full point-of-sale module with itemized billing, multi-party split payments, tax computation, and exportable PDF receipts — integrated with Stripe for secure card processing.
