Lost and Found by Pratham

Founder and creator of a travel-vlogging YouTube channel dedicated to discovering hidden gems, cultural stories, and lesser-known destinations.

The Channel Concept

"Lost and Found by Pratham" is born from a simple yet powerful idea: that the most extraordinary places and stories are often the ones hiding in plain sight. While mainstream travel content focuses on popular tourist hotspots, this channel deliberately ventures off the beaten path — seeking out forgotten temples tucked away in forests, coastal villages untouched by time, street food stalls known only to locals, and cultural traditions that are slowly fading from collective memory.

Every video is a journey of rediscovery. The "Lost" represents places, cultures, and stories that the modern world has overlooked or forgotten. The "Found" is the act of bringing them back to light — preserving them through the lens of a camera and sharing them with an audience hungry for authentic, meaningful travel experiences.

The channel serves as both a travelogue and a cultural archive, blending cinematic visuals with personal narration, local conversations, and historical context to create content that educates, inspires, and entertains.

Content Categories

The channel's content spans a rich variety of themes, each designed to offer a unique window into the diverse tapestry of places, cultures, and cuisines that make travel such a transformative experience.

🗺️ Hidden Gem Explorations 🎭 Cultural Deep Dives 🍜 Street Food Adventures 🏔️ Nature & Trekking 🏛️ Historical Walkthroughs 🌅 Cinematic Travel Films 👤 Local Story Spotlights 📸 Travel Tips & Guides

From the mist-covered peaks of the Western Ghats to the bustling lanes of historic market towns, from ancient temple festivals to quiet moments in a roadside chai stall — the channel captures the full spectrum of what it means to travel with curiosity and intention.

Equipment & Creative Tools

Creating cinematic travel content requires a careful balance between portability and quality. The production setup is designed to be travel-friendly while maintaining professional output:

Camera & Lenses: Primary camera for 4K video capture paired with versatile zoom and wide-angle lenses for landscape and close-up shots. A compact action camera handles extreme conditions and unique angles.

Stabilization: A 3-axis gimbal stabilizer ensures smooth, cinematic footage even while walking through crowded markets or hiking steep trails.

Audio: Wireless lavalier microphone for clear narration, shotgun mic for ambient sound capture, and quality headphones for on-location audio monitoring.

Post-Production: Professional video editing software for non-linear editing, colour grading tools for cinematic look development, and graphic design tools for thumbnail creation and branding.

Creative Vision

At its core, "Lost and Found by Pratham" is about more than travel — it's about storytelling. Every destination has a story, every local has a perspective, and every journey has a lesson. The channel aims to be a bridge between the viewer and the world, offering not just visual beauty but emotional depth and cultural understanding.

The long-term vision is to build a community of thoughtful travellers who approach the world with respect, curiosity, and a desire to understand rather than merely consume. Through consistent content, genuine storytelling, and a commitment to quality, the channel aspires to become a trusted voice in the travel content landscape.
